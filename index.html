About Nankana Sahib

Nankana Sahib is a historic city located in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is world-famous as the birthplace of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, the founder of Sikhism. The city holds immense spiritual importance for Sikhs and attracts thousands of pilgrims every year.

Apart from its religious significance, Nankana Sahib has developed into a growing modern city with schools, colleges, and local markets that reflect a blend of cultural heritage and progress. Visitors can explore ancient gurdwaras, taste traditional Punjabi food, and experience the warm hospitality of its residents.

The government has been continuously working to preserve its historic sites while improving facilities for tourists. Whether you are a pilgrim or a traveler, Nankana Sahib offers a peaceful environment, cultural richness, and a deep connection to history.
